Excursion in Japan

Japan is a country of many contrasts, from the snowcapped peaks of the northern Japanese Alps to the subtropical climate of Okinawa, with the cities of Tokyo, Kyoto and Osaka in between. The country is blessed with a wealth of natural beauty, from the snow-capped mountains of Hokkaido to the subtropical climate of Okinawa. It is also home to some of the world's most technologically advanced cities, such as Tokyo and Osaka.

Tokyo City

Tokyo, Japan’s busy capital, mixes the ultramodern and the traditional, from neon-lit skyscrapers to historic temples. The opulent Meiji Shinto Shrine is known for its towering gate and surrounding woods. The Imperial Palace sits amid large public gardens.
